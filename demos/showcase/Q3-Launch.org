Cumulative repeater — the next instance lands two weeks

* TODO Monthly KPI review (next-from-completion .+1m) :review:metrics:
SCHEDULED: <2026-06-01 Mon .+1m>
:PROPERTIES:
:ID: 55555555-dddd-dddd-dddd-000000000003
:RRULE: FREQ=MONTHLY
:EFFORT: 1:30
:END:
Next-from-completion repeater — the next instance schedules
one month after the user actually completes the task, not
from the original due date. Rare, but useful for things
where "30 days from when you actually did it" is the spec.

* TODO Multi-day weekly cadence (Mon + Wed + Fri) :ops:routine:
SCHEDULED: <2026-05-18 Mon ++1w>
:PROPERTIES:
:ID: 55555555-dddd-dddd-dddd-000000000004

Multi-weekday RRULE that the SCHEDULED cookie can't
express alone (Org cookies only encode FREQ + INTERVAL).

projection. Stock org-agenda renders the cookie; Atrium
reads :RRULE: on read-back.
